    How To Style A Quiff

    To style a quiff, first, apply a small amount of hair product to damp hair. Then, use a comb to create a voluminous mound at the front of your hairline. Next, blow-dry the quiff while combing it upwards to add height and shape. Finally, use a bit more product to hold the quiff in place and add texture as desired.
